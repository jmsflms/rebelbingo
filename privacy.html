Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how Rebel Bingo ("we", "us") collects and uses personal data when you sign up for updates on our website.

Who is responsible for your data

Data controller: Rebel Bingo
Contact for privacy requests: rebels@rebelbingo.com

What we collect

  • Your email address (when you submit it via our signup form).
  • We do not intentionally collect special category data (e.g., health, religion, political opinions).

Why we collect it

We use your email address to send you updates about Rebel Bingo (news, announcements, event updates).

Our legal basis

We process your email address based on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time (see “Your rights” below).

Where your data is stored

Signup submissions are stored in Google Sheets (Google Workspace / Google LLC) and may be processed using Google Apps Script to record form submissions. Google acts as a data processor on our behalf.

How long we keep your data

We keep your email address until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it, or until we decide we no longer need it for updates.

Who we share your data with

We do not sell your email address. We only share it with service providers needed to store/operate the signup system (e.g., Google).

Your rights

Depending on your location (including the UK/EU), you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request restriction of processing or object to processing (in certain cases)
  • Data portability (in certain cases)

To exercise any of these rights, email: rebels@rebelbingo.com.

Unsubscribing

You can unsubscribe at any time by emailing rebels@rebelbingo.com with the subject line “Unsubscribe”, and we will remove you.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we aim to protect your data using appropriate safeguards.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new effective date.

If you expand data collection (names, phone numbers, ads tracking, analytics, mailing platforms), you should update this policy.
